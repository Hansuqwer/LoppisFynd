Loppisfynd – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Loppisfynd is designed to be offline-first. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud features.

Data stored on your device

Loppisfynd stores the following locally:

  • Hauls (title, date, optional location you add)
  • Items (keywords, notes, prices, computed stats)
  • Draft listings (title, description, asking price)
  • Scan photos and thumbnails
  • App settings and preferences

This data is never shared with third parties.

Cloud sync (optional)

If you sign in and enable cloud sync, Loppisfynd may upload:

  • Haul and item metadata to a secure database (Supabase)
  • Scan photos to encrypted cloud storage

Cloud sync is entirely optional. The app works fully without it. All cloud data is encrypted in transit (TLS). Your photos are stored in a private bucket accessible only to your account.

Online requests

When you are online and have enabled price comparisons, the app fetches sold-price data from Tradera via a proxy. These requests contain search keywords (derived from scanned items) but no personal information.

AI identification

If you enable cloud identification, photos of scanned items are sent to a secure AI proxy for object recognition. Photos are processed in real-time and not stored on the server. You can disable this in Settings at any time.

Crash reporting

If configured, the app sends anonymous crash reports via Sentry. These contain technical diagnostic data (device model, OS version, stack traces) but no personal information, photos, or scan data.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used to capture photos of items for scanning. Photos stay on-device unless you enable cloud sync.
  • Location: Optionally used to tag hauls with the flea market location. Never shared with third parties.
  • Notifications: Used to inform you when background sync completes.
  • Internet: Required for cloud sync, price comparisons, AI identification, and crash reporting.

Data export and deletion

In the app you can:

  • Export all your data as JSON or CSV
  • Delete all local data
  • Delete all cloud data (when signed in)
  • Request full account deletion

Children

Loppisfynd is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
