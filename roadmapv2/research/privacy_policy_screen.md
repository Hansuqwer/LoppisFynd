# Privacy Policy Screen - Nature Distilled

## Purpose

- Render long-form policy text reliably.

## Layout

```
AppBar: Privacy Policy
Scroll
  - Table of contents (optional)
  - Policy text (markdown/html)
```

## Tokens (Color + Typography)

Use v2 ramps from `roadmapv2/research/color_palette_tokens.md` and text styles from `roadmapv2/research/typography_tokens.md`.

Color:
- background: neutral0
- surface: neutral1/neutral2 (opaque)
- text: ink0

Typography:
- headings: headlineLarge/headlineMedium
- body: bodyMedium
- links: electricBlue

## Notes

- Prefer no glass blur; long text over photos reduces readability.

## Asset Suggestions (roadmapv2/images)

- Prefer none (plain neutral background). If needed: `roadmapv2/images/loppis_background.png` heavily tinted/blurred.
