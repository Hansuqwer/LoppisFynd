## Signals Seen

- Mobile dashboards consistently succeed when they:
  - have one obvious primary action
  - show 2-4 headline metrics
  - provide a short, scrollable "recent" list
  - keep charts small (sparklines) unless the screen is explicitly analytics

## What To Steal For LoppisFynd

- Keep hero CTA as the dominant element.
- Keep stats grid compact (2x2) but allow one tile to become a "wide" tile when needed.
- Use bento spacing + glass surfaces; avoid adding divider lines for structure.
