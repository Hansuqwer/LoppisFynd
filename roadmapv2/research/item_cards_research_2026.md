- Shopify Polaris: Layout (space defines proximity; hierarchy via weight/contrast; avoid dividers as hierarchy)

- Behance: item card UI patterns (image-first cards, overlay actions, quick meta rows)
  - https://www.behance.net/search/projects/item%20card%20ui

## Signals Seen

- Cards win when they answer 3 questions fast:
  - what is it?
  - what is the value (price/profit)?
  - what is the next action?
- Recent work leans toward:
  - fewer visible actions (one primary, rest tucked behind long-press/overflow)
  - bolder numeric hierarchy (price/profit is visually dominant)
  - predictable chip placement (category/condition)

## What To Steal For LoppisFynd

- Make the whole card tappable for "details"; keep one visible primary action.
- Keep overlays minimal: 1 icon action (save) + optional overflow.
- Prioritize price/profit typography (Space Grotesk) over decorative elements.
